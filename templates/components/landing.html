Frequently Asked Questions

Is my medical data safe?

+
Yes. BioFlow AI runs 100% locally on your machine. Your PDF is parsed in memory, and the AI models (Ollama) run on your hardware. No data is ever sent to the cloud or third-party servers.

What hardware do I need?

+
You need a computer capable of running Ollama. We recommend at least 8GB of RAM (16GB preferred) and a decent CPU or GPU. Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3) works exceptionally well.

What labs can I upload?

+
The system is optimized for standard blood panels (CBC, CMP, Lipid Panel, Hormones) from major providers like Quest Diagnostics, LabCorp, and hospital systems. It reads text-based PDFs.
